What is debugging?

Introduction

  • Debugging is the process of finding and resolving bugs
    • Bugs are defects or problems that prevent correct operation
  • Running the application shows us the effects (or symptoms) of errors
    • Not the cause of errors
  • It can be hard to identify and pin down problems
    • Remember the computer is only doing what it is told

Classes of errors

  • Syntax or type errors
    • Always caught and reported by the compiler
  • Typos or simple errors
    • Passes type-check
    • Easy to fix when identified
  • Implementation errors
    • Wrong use of framework
  • Logical errors
    • Logically flawed algorithms

Ask questions the smart way

  • Describe the symptoms of your problem
    • Be careful and clear
  • Describe your own research
    • What did you try to do to understand the problem?
  • Describe the diagnostic steps you took
    • What did you do to pin down the problem?
  • Provide a way to reproduce the problem
    • In a runnable, controlled environment
